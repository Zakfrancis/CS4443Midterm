Bill Gates Mr. Robot Elon Musk
After retiring from his extensive work at Microsoft, Professor Gates wanted to give back to the students. He teaches courses on operating systems and business systems programming. Mr. Robot is a world renown white hat hacker (mostly). He now teaches cryptography and network design to students looking for jobs in the security field. Professor Musk is still busy at work with his multiple ventures, but graciously teaches a few classes in his limited off time. He mainly teaches hands-on engineering courses and students love him because he gives the top student a Tesla at the end of each semester.

After solving the problem of time travel, Dr. Hackerman has come from the year 1987 to teach students the practical power of programming. His mainstay classes are computer archetecture and languages such as COBOL Professor Snowden sadly is limited to online classes only at this moment due to certain legal troubles.
