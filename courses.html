Take a look at some of our wonderful courses!

  • Computer Science I
    This course is your first step into the world of computer science. Learn the syntax of c++ as well as many of the programming conventions you will take with you for life.
  • "Ethical" Hacking
    Truely an exciting course if there ever was one. Join in to learn the ins and outs of networking and social engineering. Watch out though, if you get caught by law enforcement, that'll be an automatic failure!

    Learn the wonderful world of email scams, how to avoid them, and more importantly, how to effectively earn some money. Professor Bubbles will teach you the skills you need to know to begin paying those student loans back before you graduate. Take it from him, he knows how to catch a fish!
  • Web Design
    Learn from the best on how to make attractive and effective websites. Make sure to take this class seriously and definitly don't make any jokes about the department and you will do great.
